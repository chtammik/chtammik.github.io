Home

I'm a Sound Designer for games. I shout at microphones and record in the field. I passionately mangle and twist sounds into existence. My sound design aims to pass valuable information on to the player, evoke emotion and assist in world building. My passion lies with that magical moment where sound meets code. I create sounds and implement in engine - even code my own audio tools. Also I love interacting with human beings.
