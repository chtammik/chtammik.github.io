About

My sound career started with a classical training in playing the Clarinet but during high school I discovered my passion for electronic music. After dabbling in DJ-ing I soon started getting into music production and sound design. After working in advertising for some years I decided to study post production for. During my studies at Vancouver Film School I deiscovered game audio as my passion and focused on developing my implementation skills.

I am experienced in Audiokinetic's Wwise and Firelight's Fmod Studio Implementation Software and I have worked with both the Unity and the Unreal Game Engines.

I am knowledgable about all areas of the film production cycle including Production Sound & ADR Recording, Sound Effects Editing, Sound Design, Foley and Mixing.

Allerdings

Allerdings is an experimental project for me to play with musical ideas, work with sound in new ways and dive deep into the possibilities of Digital Signal Processing and Synthesis.
Most of these experiments are published on my Soundcloud profile.
