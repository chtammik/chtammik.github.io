About

I am a Sound Designer, with a passion for game audio - that magic place where sound design meets code. I am experienced in Audiokinetic's Wwise and Firelight's Fmod Studio Middleware and I have worked with both the Unity and Unreal Game Engines.

I am also knowledgeable in usic composition (interactive music) as well as all areas of the film production cycle including Production Sound & ADR Recording, Sound Effects Editing, Sound Design, Foley, Surround-Sound Mixing & delivery procedures.

My sound career started with my a classical training in playing the Clarinet. During high school I discovered my passion for electronic music and after dabbling in DJ-ing I soon started developing my skills in music production and sound design. After working in advertising for two and a half years I decided to study post production for film. My studies at Vancouver Film School introduced me to game audio and I discovered my true passion. This became very obvious as I participated in many Game Jams. Collaborating with a small team and developing afully functioning game with limited resources and a harsh deadline. I thrive on the interaction with other human beings.

On my Soundcloud profile I play with musical ideas, work with sound in new ways and dive deep into the possibilities of Digital Signal Processing and Synthesis. It also serves as a platform for music I write at game jams, sound-scapes and other miscellaneous sound projects I work on.
