Film / Video


Need For Speed: Shift 2 (24hour Sound Re-Design) - more info at Vimeo

This project was realized within a 24 hour period with limited resources and mixed in LCRS surround sound. To create vaiations of the 36 sound files that were provided with the video I used the KYMA sound design engine and the FireworX hardware unit among other processing techniques.


Emotive Game Trailer - “WoW - Wrath of the Lich King” - more info at Vimeo

With limited music tracks I created a soundscape supporting the cinematic visual of the World of Warcraft expansion trailer to "Wrath Of The Lich King".

Game Audio


Sanity Music System in Wwise - more info at Vimeo

For my term 5 game audio assignment I, together with four other sound designers created a 25 minute experience with branching story lines. This is a short overview of the dynamic music system I created out of stems with randomized sub tracks, tempo synced transition elements as well as playlist containers with differnt logic behavior.


TIB Music System in Wwise - more info at Vimeo

In collaboration with other sound designers and the students at the Vancouver Film School Game design Campus we created a 3v3 moba game. I composed the original music and implemented in Wwise. The music is randomized in multiple layers and the instrumentation changes based on the maximum amout of point any team has at any given point in time.
